THE IDEA:

Started in 2014 by two brothers from Stellenosch, Amoija Events was created out of love for sport and the great outdoors. The vision was always simple- create premier endurance events, inspired by the majestic mountains of the Western Cape.


THE DIFFERENCE:

Events held on exclusive routes, available only to Amoija Entrants.

Amoija also organises 3rd party events. Highly skilled and experienced guns for hire which can help bring your vision onto the trails.


OUR MISSION:

Amoija aims to give athletes a testing endurance challenge, backed by an unrivaled event experience.
